Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Pixel Chain, a retro puzzle game built with privacy in mind. We are committed to protecting your gaming experience. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you play our game on Android and iOS devices.

Information We Collect

We value your privacy. Pixel Chain does not collect any personal identifiers such as your real name, email address, phone number, location, or payment info.

To support core game features, we process only the following anonymous gameplay data:

  • Anonymous Profile Data: When you set up the game, a randomly selected profile icon and your chosen custom player name are stored. No real-world identities are requested or saved.
  • Game Progression: We save your max unlocked level (from Level 1 to 100) and your collection of unlocked sticker rewards to sync your progress.
  • Device Identification: An anonymous, system-generated identifier is used to unique-link your offline progress with cloud storage backups.

🔒 Firebase Cloud Synchronization

Pixel Chain utilizes Google Firebase Firestore to securely back up your level progress and unlocked sticker rewards. This ensures that you can restore your gameplay achievements even if you reinstall the app or change devices. All data is linked to an anonymous credential.

Storage & Data Retention

Your gameplay data is stored in two locations:

  • Local Storage: Achievements and settings are cached on your device using React Native's secure storage system for seamless offline play.
  • Cloud Database: Game saves are backed up to secure Google Firebase servers.

If you want to clear your data, you can do so at any time using the Reset Progress or Delete Profile options directly from the in-game Account screen, which permanently wipes both your local and cloud saves.

Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device:

  • Google Firebase Auth & Firestore: To manage anonymous user login sessions and progression state sync.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Services: To facilitate game download, updates, and native OS notifications.

Children's Privacy

Pixel Chain is designed for players of all ages and is fully family-friendly. Because we do not collect any personal information (such as real names or contact details), we do not knowingly solicit or collect data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Any updates are effective immediately upon posting to this URL.
